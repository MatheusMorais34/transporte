# **App Name**: TransitFlow

## Core Features:

- Service Overview: Showcase the range of transportation services offered (e.g., freight, logistics, express delivery).
- AI-Powered Route Optimization Tool: AI powered tool to provide efficient and cost-effective delivery routes to clients.
- Real-Time Tracking: Enable customers to track their shipments in real time using a tracking number.
- Client Testimonials: Display testimonials from satisfied clients to build trust and credibility.
- Contact Form: Allow potential clients to easily reach out for inquiries or quotes.
- Blog/News Section: Share industry insights, company news, and helpful tips related to transportation and logistics.
- Quote Request Form: Enable potential clients to request a customized quote for their specific transportation needs.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep sky blue (#3EB489) for trust and reliability, reflecting the efficient transportation services offered.
- Background color: Very light cyan (#E0F4F1), providing a clean and professional backdrop that emphasizes clarity and openness.
- Accent color: Soft lime green (#A1D6B7) to complement the primary color, adding a fresh and inviting feel.
- Font pairing: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for headlines, lending a precise, contemporary feel, and 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text.
- Use clean, professional icons to represent different services and features, ensuring clarity and ease of understanding.
- Maintain a clean and organized layout with clear calls to action, guiding visitors through the site effortlessly.
- Incorporate subtle animations to highlight key information and enhance user engagement, without being distracting.
